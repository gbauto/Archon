Rules applied (audit before sending)

Per framework-cheatsheet: - First touch opens with a calibrated question, not a hook (Voss) - Mid-sequence includes one labeling statement — "It sounds like…" (Voss) - Final touch uses a no-oriented close — "Is it a ridiculous idea…" (Voss) - Physician/owner is the hero; PMC is the guide (Miller SB7) - At least one touch names an alternative PMC displaces (Dunford) - Subject lines: 3–5 words, no question marks - Precise odd numbers only — bracketed [FILL] where specialty-audit data is not yet in the knowledge base - "15+ years" is not referenced (quarterly cap preserved) - Give-first cadence: three touches of context/proof before any explicit ask (Vaynerchuk) - "Hook" = calibrated question exposing a blind spot. Never fear-hook. See retired examples in apollo-sequence#retired-versions-historical.

Pre-send checklist (run on the imported Apollo list)

  • [ ] Dedup against active BRT sequences — dual-brand rule #1 (businesses/pmc/strategy/dual-brand-audit). Zero overlap.
  • [ ] Dedup against master suppression list (prior disconnects, rejects, angry replies).
  • [ ] Filter to email_status = valid (52 of 77). Do not send to unknown or blank.
  • [ ] For generic inboxes (info@, admin@, contact@) — either enrich with a named-DM email from Apollo, or drop. Never send the personalized sequence to a shared inbox.
  • [ ] Confirm [FirstName] / Dr. [LastName] tokens populate from the right columns (the CSV has #1 DM as the contact name — split into first/last for Apollo).
  • [ ] All placeholders [FILL: N] / [FILL: …] resolved with real audit data, OR rewritten to a pattern-only variant. Per framework-cheatsheet — precise odd numbers come from real audits, never from imagination.
  • [ ] Subject lines audited: 3–5 words, no question mark, no duplicate across the 4 touches.
  • [ ] Segment assignment verified — no chiro lead got the medspa sequence and vice versa. The CSV Type column is the source of truth.
  • [ ] Tier-based send order: send Hot rows first, Warm rows on a second wave 2 weeks later.

Reply handling

Reply type Move
Positive ("yes / send a time") Calendar link immediately. Stop sequence.
Objection ("not now / we're fine / have a consultant") One labeling statement from discovery-banks. Pause 30 days.
Curious ("what do you do exactly?") One-pager from landing-page. Resume sequence.
Generic inbox auto-responder Drop from sequence, mark bounced-generic, add to suppression.
Unsubscribe / angry Honor immediately, cross-channel suppression.

Metrics that matter

Per apollo-sequence discipline — reply rate is vanity. Track:

  1. Calls booked per 100 sent (benchmark 2–4%)
  2. "That's right" hit rate on the resulting discovery calls (target >50%)
  3. Segment win-rate delta — does the Chiro sequence outperform Medspa, or vice versa? After 30 days of data, double down on the winner.
